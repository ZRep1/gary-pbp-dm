Gary Privacy Policy

Gary is designed to help Discord communities create and manage tabletop roleplaying experiences.

Information Stored

Gary may store information necessary to provide campaign features, including Discord user IDs, server information, character data, campaign information, preferences, and settings.

How Information Is Used

Stored information is used to provide campaign management, maintain continuity between sessions, and improve Gary's functionality.

Data Requests

Users may request information about stored data or request deletion of their data.

Third-Party Services

Gary may use third-party services such as AI providers or hosting services to provide functionality.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, please contact the Gary development team.
